Coca Cola CSR Approach

As a market leader, Nokia feels that one of the best ways they can contribute to the global community is to conduct business in a responsible way, writes Sarah Tahir.

The company that gained massive popularity with the tagline, “We call this human technology”, coined in 1997, has till now, successfully retained the human element in all their business operations. So far, Nokia’s concern is not only for consumers, but for society at large, and has increased in its depth and breadth over time.
